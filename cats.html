In the animal justice system, feline-based offenses are considered especially heinous. In Sunderland, the dedicated detectives who investigate these vicious felonies are members of an elite squad known as the Special Victims Unit. These are their stories.

Salvatore is a 5-6 year old black male domestic shorthair. Wilson is a 1 year old tuxedo male of mixed fur length.
